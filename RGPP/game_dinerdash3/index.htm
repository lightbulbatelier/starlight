Diner Dash: Flo on the Go
She started a franchise business from the ground up and saved the locals from the cruel claws of progress and greed. Now it's time to take a well reserved break. That should be the idea. Facing bad service and a lost baggage at a cruise, Flo will hit two targets with a stone on vacations.

Notes & Known Issues
On Windows 10, to enjoy all the features of this game, you must install a non-bombed version of Adobe Flash Player (ActiveX).

Please be advised to keep your graphic card drivers updated to avoid visual glitches and crashes.
 
Some games are still not tested under ReactOS and Wine. Please report any issues at the official RGPP Reddit page.

Summary
The objective for this challenge is as easy as pie: seat your customers, take their orders and serve the most delicious dishes at the speed of light. More satisfaction means more tips for you. You can improve the aesthetic of the on-board restaurants with several upgrades.

Just like Diner Dash and Diner Dash 2, keep it up the same drill: keep your customers as satisfied as possible and the tips will be generous.

This game will not be like the previous versions, with interchangeable outfits and dark mode levels.

—Nicolas Bahamondes
