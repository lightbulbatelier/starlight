TOP ARCADE QUESTIONS

Why can't RealArcade tell that I'm online?

You may have an older version of RealArcade.

First, download the newest version of RealArcade at www.realonearcade.com and install it. If you are still having the same problem with the new version, you may need to adjust your online settings in RealArcade. Follow these steps:

  1. Launch RealArcade
  2. Click on Menu in the top right corner
  3. Select Preferences from the drop down menu
  4. Click on the Internet tab.
  5. Select "Always assume I'm online" at the bottom of the menu
