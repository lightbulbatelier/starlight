The recreation of RealArcade's original game guide is finally here! Starlight is a service that comprises two important elements:

• A proprietary toolkit developed by AstralSoup to create a brand-new RealArcade games portal that directly feeds with the RealArcade Games Preservation Project, specifically the RGS game installer files.

• The community-driven contents that are property of their respective volunteers and curators, like a Wiki with full names in front.
 
No matter if you prefer an offline or online copy of our game guide, both flavors are already covered. Our service is transparent in nature, you can study our sources or fork it.
Connect with other fans of RealArcade on Discord and Reddit. Here you can also find the latest service releases of RGPP. Their opinions and reviews have a space on Starlight, too.
