Multiplayer Help

Using Multiplayer Matching

Some games offer multiplayer matching. This feature allows you to play the game with others over the Internet. Multiplayer matching involves four steps:

Step 1 - Type your nickname

When you click on the Multiplayer Tab on the Game Page, you will be prompted for a nickname. Choose a name to identify yourself that's not your real name. This will be your nickname as you play.

Step 2 - Enter the Game Lobby

After entering your nickname, you will be placed in the Game Lobby. The lobby is like the lobby of an office or hotel. It's the first place all people come to before they break off into groups. From the lobby, you can chat with other players, see who's interested in a game, or you can leave the lobby and go on to step 3. Remember to use proper netiquette when chatting with others.

Step 3 - Enter a Game Room

When you are ready to play a game, click on a room to enter the room. If the game you are about to play requires a CDROM, be sure the CDROM is in the computer and ready to be accessed. Finally, click the I'm Ready button in the upper right hand corner. Note that you won't see this button until you go in a room.

Step 4 - Play the game

When the host of the game room is ready to play, he or she will launch the game on everyone's computer. And then, you're off!

If the game is peer-to-peer, you can also create your own room and have others join you. To create a game room of your own, click Create Game Room. Note that you cannot create a game room if you are behind a firewall.
