Bulletin News  April 26th, 2021
JOEY SAYS UPDATE!

A new update for BlackBox SDK was published on GitHub. Please download and apply it on your RealArcade deployment as soon as possible. This update contains the latest Game Page Template 0.3.1alpha, which may be the last one before going beta.

As always, please backup your own game pages and delete your HTML folder before applying the patch to your RealArcade folder.

As a bonus, the registry files to activate or deactivate the bulletin board on startup will be included in the new BlackBox SDK package.


I sincerely hope you enjoy this release and expect more updates soon! (Aaaaand we are moving the date of the bulletin at the top right corner of this window.
