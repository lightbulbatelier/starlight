RealArcade®: Customer Communications and Your Privacy

RealArcade is an application that allows you to manage and organize your PC game collection and to easily find, download, and play downloadable games from the Internet.

What Information is Sent to RealNetworks, Its Partners, or Third Party Sites?

RealArcade has many product functions and communications features that allow you to manage your PC game collection, chat, engage in multiplayer games, participate in the online community of game players, get more information about your games, receive the latest product updates, and get new games from other companies.  These features may require you to provide certain personal information for limited purposes to RealNetworks.  By accepting this policy statement, you understand that these product functions and communications features are governed by the RealNetworks privacy policy.

The following product functions and communications features are discussed below:

  • Advertising Windows
  • Cookies
  • Electronic Registration
  • Fan Sites, Third Party Download Sites, and Other 3rd Party Links
  • Find Installed Games
  • Message Boards/Ratings & Reviews
  • Multiplayer Matching and Chat
  • My Account
  • Number of Times Played
  • Scores Reporting
  • Share this Game with a Friend
  • Software Updates

How This Information is Used

Advertising Windows

RealArcade may contain advertisements and "InfoWindows" that are sent dynamically from our server when the product is used. Like the banner ads on many Web sites, these advertisements are delivered via an ad management service ÷ currently DoubleClick ÷ that may use cookies to ensure that you see relevant advertisements. You may click here to view DoubleClick's privacy policy and to opt out of receiving cookies from DoubleClick. When you click on an ad, RealNetworks is notified that this ad was selected, in order for RealNetworks to create aggregate and anonymous reports on user traffic patterns for advertisers and partners. RealArcade advertising windows sometimes link to other Web sites. RealNetworks is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those Web sites. For more information on RealNetworks relationship with third party advertising management companies, please click here.

Cookies

RealArcade transmits optional Internet Explorer cookies in three ways:

  1. When RealArcade is used, our advertising system delivers an anonymous cookie that indicates which "InfoWindow" was displayed in the product. This cookie is used to ensure that the next time you use RealArcade, the "InfoWindow" that you see is updated.
  2. RealArcade loads a Web page when it displays Advertising Windows (see below). These Web pages use microsoft's Internet Explorer web browser, and as a result, automatically transmit any pre-existing Internet Explorer cookies to the Web page content provider. In some cases, this may be a RealNetworks web page (for example, when a real.com guide page is displayed while you download, any pre-existing real.com cookies are transmitted). RealNetworks does not use cookies to associate download information with your personal information.
  3. If you are downloading from a Web site that uses cookies, RealArcade will temporarily store and pass on these cookies to your browser. RealArcade does not edit the cookies sent by the download Web site, and these cookies are not sent to RealNetworks.

You may disable these cookies by turning off your Internet Explorer browser cookies. Your browser does not have to accept cookies in order for RealArcade to function. For more information on RealNetworks' use of cookies, please click here. 

Electronic Registration

During electronic registration, you can optionally send information about your location, e-mail address, game genre preference, and connection speed. This information is sent with relevant system and player version information. The e-mail address is used in the event that you elect to receive updates and special offers via e-mail from RealNetworks. The other information is used for aggregate statistical analysis and to provide content that may be of interest to you. If you do not wish to fill out an electronic registration, choose "Remind me to register later" during registration-- a registration reminder will appear three times, after which you may choose to permanently disable registration reminders.

Fan Sites, 3rd Party Download Sites, and Other 3rd Party Links

RealArcade offers access to game fan sites and other related game, developer, and publisher websites within RealArcade, as well as other third party game download sites not managed by RealNetworks. Some of these sites are hosted and managed by GameSpy Industries and others are managed by other independent companies.  RealNetworks does not share any personal information with these sites without your consent. These sites may have their own logins and registration and may use cookies to maintain information about you, to track site usage, to track the viewing of advertising, or for other reasons specific to the site. RealNetworks will use this Cookie information only as described above (see "Cookies"). You may voluntarily choose to view these sites in RealArcade. By choosing to view these sites and download information or games from them, you agree to the privacy policies of each independent site.  To view the GameSpy privacy policies for game "Planet" sites managed by them, click here.

Find Installed Games

During RealArcade startup (if you select "Yes" to have RealArcade find games on your system) or when you select the "Find Installed Games" option under the Menu button, RealArcade will search your hard drive for games that are installed on your system. RealArcade maintains a database of game signatures that allows it to discover the games on your system. This information is used only to allow the game to be played, managed, and uninstalled through RealArcade and is not reported back to RealNetworks. If you do not want RealArcade to find the games on your system, you can select "No" when prompted at startup time.

Message Boards/Ratings & Reviews

RealArcade allows you to participate in the game forums & message boards on Real.com by posting messages to the boards and reading messages from other players. By posting a message to the message boards, you agree to allow RealNetworks to display your message content as well as any user profile information that you voluntarily create when you register with the message boards (unless posted anonymously) on Real.com and within RealArcade.

RealArcade also allows you to rate games and to read the game reviews of others. By submitting a review or game rating to Real.com, you agree to allow RealNetworks to display your game review information as well as your user name, location, and favorite games that you voluntarily enter on the review submittal form on our "User Ratings" pages.  

RealNetworks will not use this information for any other purpose, nor will we sell, rent or give away your personal information without your consent. RealNetworks reserves the right to not post any message or any game review for any reason. Posts and game reviews are subject to editorial approval and may be edited if deemed inappropriate for any reason. 

RealArcade occasionally points to message boards hosted by third parties, other than RealNetworks. These include message boards hosted by GameSpy Industries as well as other third party companies. Posting messages and personal information to those message boards is done at the user's own discretion and falls under the privacy policies of those individual sites. Third party sites may share this information with other companies or individuals and postings there may result in unsolicited mailings from those parties. RealNetworks does not condone or endorse posting to these sites, but merely provides access to those message boards for convenience only.

Multiplayer Matching and Chat

RealArcade offers a chat service and player matching capability that lets you find other people with whom you can play your multiplayer games. RealArcade uses underlying matchmaking and chat technology from GameSpy Industries. The matchmaking and chat services require you to enter a nickname with which you will be identified in the chat and game rooms. RealArcade sends this nickname to GameSpy backend services solely for this purpose and as outlined in the "Scores Reporting" section below. Apart from your selected user nickname, RealNetworks does not share any personally identifiable information with GameSpy Industries.  

By voluntarily creating a nickname and choosing to participate in multiplayer matching and chat within RealArcade, you agree to allow RealNetworks to display your nickname and a ping time indicating the quality of the connection between your system and other possible players and to share that nickname and ping information with GameSpy Industries, other players using RealArcade, and players using GameSpy Arcade, another gaming client, from GameSpy Industries. RealArcade does not share other account information or your e-mail address with GameSpy Industries.  

RealArcade periodically sends an anonymous, activity "ping" to GameSpy Industries when you are online that allows RealArcade users and GameSpy Arcade users to see how many people are currently playing games through the service. This information is used only for display purposes in the RealArcade and GameSpy Arcade matchmaking areas to indicate the size of the online community.  

The chat services are monitored and moderated by GameSpy Industries and are subject to the Privacy Policies of GameSpy Industries. RealNetworks is not responsible for the privacy practices of any of the GameSpy technology or the content of the chat services offered by GameSpy Industries including objectionable language or content. Participation in multiplayer matching and chat is entirely voluntary. To view the GameSpy privacy policies, please click here.

My Account

RealArcade offers convenient access to your RealNetworks account information. When you establish an account with RealNetworks, the information that you provide to us on the forms (including credit card number) will be stored by RealNetworks in order to facilitate product purchases and to respond to any support needs you may have. If you have browser cookies enabled, we will store some of your information in a cookie on your computer so that we can identify you when you visit RealNetworks' Web sites, either directly or through RealArcade, and play or download content from our sites. Cookies let you make purchases or access personalized pages without re-entering your account information. You may disable cookies in your browser if you do not want RealNetworks' Web sites to recognize your computer when you visit. As stated in RealNetworks Privacy Policy, RealNetworks does not share, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent unless compelled by law or court order to do so.

Number of Times Played

RealArcade tracks the number of times each game has been played since RealArcade was installed. This information is provided for your convenience only and is not reported back to or tracked by RealNetworks.

Scores Reporting

Certain games within RealArcade allow you to voluntarily report your scores and other game statistics to central servers managed by GameSpy Industries for the purpose of creating and maintaining global high score lists and ranking systems. You may OPT IN to this service on a per game basis. RealArcade and these select games will only send your nickname and relevant scores and game statistics to these central servers. RealArcade does not send any other personal information to GameSpy or these servers. By choosing to OPT IN to this service, you agree to allow RealNetworks and GameSpy Industries to post your nickname, scores, and game statistics on the game's web site. To view the GameSpy privacy policies, click here.

If you select to use this feature, your e-mail address will be used in the FROM part of the message and as part of the message text so that your friend will know who sent them the message. Your friendâs e-mail address will be sent from your system to a server managed by RealNetworks from which the e-mail message will be constructed and sent. Your friend will not be added to any RealNetworks database or distribution list without your friendâs permission. Neither your e-mail address nor your friendâs e-mail address will be used by RealNetworks for any other purpose.

Software Updates

RealArcade can check for the availability of software updates to ensure that you have the latest product improvements. This includes updates to RealArcade itself, as well as games that are built for RealArcade. When RealArcade checks for the availability of a software update, basic information about the product version and installed components are anonymously sent to RealNetworks to determine whether new free software is available for download. RealArcade can automatically ask for permission every month to check for the availability of software updates. This feature can be disabled by clicking on the Menu button and selecting: Preferences... Internet... Options... RealArcade Update Check.

Technical Support

For certain errors that may occur when you are using RealArcade, a dialog box will appear describing the error. When you select the button on the dialog box to obtain more information about the problem, RealArcade sends anonymous relevant system configuration information to help RealNetworks Technical Support understand the types of systems experiencing the problem. This feature can be disabled by clicking on the Menu button and selecting: Preferences... Internet... Options... Technical Support Information.

"Update Game News" Automatic Lookup

If you are connected to the Internet when RealArcade is started, RealArcade, by default, checks to see if there are updates available for the following:

  • Content (for example: Game Guide, Online Community)
  • Catalog of downloadable games
  • User interface configuration
  • Library of known games
  • Updates to the RealArcade software which may include bug fixes or enhanced functionality

During this check, RealArcade sends anonymous information about the version of RealArcade you are running, your system configuration, and certain preferences you have set. This information includes:

  • RealArcade version, Catalog version, and Content version
  • RealArcade product type (Free or Full)
  • Operating system and language
  • Distribution code
  • Connection speed
  • Favorite game type
  • Country/region and language preference

RealNetworks does not gather any personally identifiable information during this communication. Automatic Game Guide and Game Catalog Updates can be disabled by clicking on the Menu button and selecting: Preferences... Internet... Options... Game News Updates.

RealNetworks will use this Cookie information only as described above (see "Cookies"). You may voluntarily choose to participate in these sites from within RealArcade. By choosing to use these gaming services, you agree to the Privacy Policies of each independent site. RealArcade does not share any personally identifiable information with these sites without your consent.
